The Challenge

Jewellery Retail is Changing

Customers discover jewellery on Instagram, Pinterest, and Google — but most jewellery stores still lack intelligent digital tools. The gap is growing.

Discovery Has Shifted Online

70% of jewellery buyers start their journey on social media and search engines. Without AI-powered discovery, retailers miss these high-intent shoppers.

No Way to "Try" Online

Jewellery is deeply personal. Without virtual try-on, online shoppers can't visualize how pieces look on them — leading to hesitation and abandoned carts.

No Intelligent Guidance

Customers can't ask "Gold necklace under ₹50,000" and get instant answers. Traditional catalogues lack the conversational, personalized shopping experience they expect.

Built for jewellery retailers who want to compete with the biggest brands in the market — without needing a massive tech team.

Capabilities

AI Tools That Transform
Jewellery Shopping

Five powerful AI modules — each designed to make jewellery shopping effortless, engaging, and conversion-friendly.

Virtual Try-On

Customers can try rings, necklaces, and earrings directly using their phone camera. Real-time AR overlays let them see exactly how each piece looks — driving confidence and reducing returns.

AI Shopping Assistant

Customers ask questions like "Gold necklace under ₹50,000" and receive instant, curated recommendations. A conversational AI that knows your catalogue inside out.

Photo Search Jewellery

Upload an image — from Instagram, Pinterest, or a photo — and find visually similar jewellery designs from your catalogue. AI-powered visual search that converts inspiration into purchase.

Face-Based Recommendation

AI analyzes face shape and suggests the best-matching jewellery styles — the perfect earring shape, necklace length, or headpiece design for every unique face.

AI Budget Builder

Customers enter a budget and occasion — AI generates a complete, coordinated jewellery set within their price range. Smart upselling that feels like expert curation.
