The Problem

Hotels are bleeding revenue
to outdated operations.

While Marriott and Taj invest hundreds of millions in tech, independent hotels still run on paper registration cards, lost keycards, and phone-tag between departments.

5-Minute Check-In Queues

73% of travelers now expect contactless options. Manual registration frustrates guests and tanks first impressions.

Keycard Chaos

₹3–8 lakh/year on replacements. Guests locked out at midnight. Demagnetized cards are the #1 front-desk complaint.

No 24/7 Guest Support

Night-shift queries go unanswered. No multilingual support for international guests. Service recovery opportunities lost.

Paper Menus & Slow F&B

Can't update prices in real-time. Waitstaff bottleneck kills table turnover. No upsell intelligence.

Siloed Staff Operations

Housekeeping, maintenance, front desk on separate systems. Guest requests acknowledged but never fulfilled.

Manual GST Billing

Invoices by hand. Payment reconciliation takes hours. No digital receipts. WhatsApp invoice? Forget it.

01 — CHECK-IN

Smart Pre-Check-In

Guests receive a WhatsApp/SMS link 48 hours before arrival. Upload ID, sign digitally, set preferences — arrive and walk straight to the room.

  • Digital registration with e-signature
  • Government ID upload + verification
  • Advance payment or card authorization
02 — ACCESS

Contactless Room Access

Mobile key via BLE or NFC — works through Apple Wallet and Google Wallet. No app download. Auto-activates on check-in, revokes on checkout.

  • Multi-zone: room, gym, pool, elevator
  • Remote key share via WhatsApp
  • Real-time door event audit trail
03 — DINING

QR-Based F&B Ordering

Guests scan a QR code, browse a visual menu with photos and allergen info, customize, and pay — no waiter needed.

  • Auto-route orders to kitchen display
  • 86'd items hidden in real-time
  • Room-charge or pay-now billing
04 — PAYMENTS

Unified Payments & Invoicing

UPI (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm), cards, net banking, wallets. GST-compliant invoices auto-generated and WhatsApp-delivered.

  • UPI QR embedded on every invoice
  • Auto-reconciliation to guest folio
  • GSTR-1 export ready for your CA
05 — AI CONCIERGE

AI-Powered Concierge

WhatsApp-first virtual concierge handles 85%+ of routine queries in 37+ languages. Book spa, order food, request towels — via chat.

  • Proactive upsell at right moment
  • Auto-escalation to human staff
  • PMS-integrated personalization
06 — OPERATIONS

Staff Automation Hub

Housekeeping auto-assignment, maintenance ticketing with SLA, cross-department task board, and performance dashboards.

  • Voice-to-task in 200+ languages
  • Room status synced to PMS live
  • Preventive maintenance schedules

Why Atithi OS

Because "Atithi Devo Bhava"
deserves modern tools.

Every decision — from payment rails to communication channels — is designed for how Indian hotels actually operate. Not a Western product retrofitted.

India-First Architecture

UPI-native payments, GST-compliant invoicing, WhatsApp-first guest communication, Aadhaar-ready ID verification. Built on Indian rails from day one.

Works Offline & On-Premise

Critical operations — check-in, door access, task routing — work offline and sync when connected. On-premise deployments ensure zero dependency on internet for core hotel operations. Built for Cherrapunji, Shimla, Munnar — not just metro cities.

Deploy Your Way — Cloud or On-Premise

Run Atithi OS on our managed cloud, your own private cloud, or entirely on-premise behind your firewall — just like any enterprise-grade system. Your data stays exactly where you want it. Integrates with existing PMS (Opera, Hotelogix, Cloudbeds, eZee) and locks with zero disruption.
